HOTELS

Stay at the Asian Side

If you're looking for a quiter, more relaxing stay, a hotel on the Asian side of Istanbul is the best option. This side of the city tends to be more affordable as well. The Bosphorus Palace Hotel, and many other hotels that contain "Palace" are historical sites from the Ottoman Empire that have been converted into hotels. There are more palace hotels in the European side, but there tends to be more touristic traffic there. If you'd like an authentic experience where it will be easy to commute, the Asian side is a better selection. The airport on the Asian side is called Sabiha Gokcen, so if you'd like to be closer to the airport, make sure to see how far your hotel is before.

Stay at the European Side

Booking.com
If you are more interested in seeing as many sites as you can and don't mind a lively atmosphere, the European side is certainly more suitable for your interests. Most museums and historical sites like the Hagia Sophia and Blue Mosque are on the European side and the hotels there are more on the higher-end of luxury and prices. If you'd like to feel like royalty, booking a hotel from one of the exquisite options in the European side of the city will allow you to live in a palace like true royalty. The Istanbul more commonly advertised is certainly more in the European side, so booking a hotel in Europe as a first-time tourist in the city may be a better choice. The newly constructed airport, the Istanbul Airport, will be best for flights as it is in the European side as well.
